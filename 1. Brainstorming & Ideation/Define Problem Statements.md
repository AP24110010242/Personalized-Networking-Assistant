# Define Problem Statements

### The Problem
Professionals, researchers, and students frequently attend networking events, conferences, and meetups where they need to make meaningful connections. However:
1. **Conversation Anxiety:** Many individuals struggle to start conversations or break the ice with strangers.
2. **Lack of Context:** Attendees often fail to connect event themes with their personal interests on the spot.
3. **Factual Uncertainty:** Users might want to discuss trending topics (e.g., blockchain, green energy) but lack quick, verified context, leading to hesitation.

### The Problem Statement
"How might we design a lightweight, easy-to-use AI tool that extracts event themes, generates context-aware conversation starters based on user interests, and verifies facts in real-time, helping attendees network confidently?"
