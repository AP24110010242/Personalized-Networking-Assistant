# Empathy Map - Networking Attendees

### 👁️ SAYS
- "I want to network, but I don't know what to say first."
- "I'm interested in green energy, but how do I bring it up at a smart cities event?"
- "Is it true that blockchain is used in healthcare diagnostics?"

### 🧠 THINKS
- "Everyone else seems so confident."
- "I hope I don't say something factually incorrect."
- "I need a quick cheat sheet of icebreakers."

### 🏃 DOES
- Stands near the food counter instead of talking to people.
- Spends too much time on their phone looking up event details.
- Sticks only to people they already know.

### 🎭 FEELS
- **Anxiety:** Nervous about starting conversations.
- **Intimidation:** Surrounded by experts.
- **Desire:** Wants to make valuable professional connections.
